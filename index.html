What is an Average Joe?

The term "Average Joe" refers to a regular, everyday person. Someone who represents the "average" individual in society. An Average Joe might be the guy next door, the person working a 9-to-5, grabbing coffee, cheering for their favorite team, or just trying to make it through the day. The term is a nod to humility and normalcy, celebrating the beauty in the ordinary.
